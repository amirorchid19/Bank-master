    Age

    Here’s why it is crucial to replace your debit/credit card by 31 Dec

    The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has recently directed all banks to replace existing magnetic stripe-only credit/debit cards by the end of December 2018. These cards will become obsolete after this time period. The cards will have to be replaced with EMV (Europay, Mastercard, Visa) chip cards.The directive to move to EMV cards is applicable for domestic and international cards. The cards will be replaced without any additional cost for the cardholder. Chip-based cards have a higher level of security when compared to magnetic stripe-only cards, as these have better standards of data encryption and storage. These cards also mitigate the risks of cloning or skimming, hence reducing frauds.
